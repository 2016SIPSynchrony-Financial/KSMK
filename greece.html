Pebbles Players

What You'll Need:

You need a base, which can be a tree, wall, or post. Then you need a pebble, which can be a stone, a marble, or a button.

How to play:

Players stand in line shoulder to shoulder facing the player that has the pebble that player has the pebble in one hand and moves down the line pretending to place the pebble in each hand. When the pebble actually changes hands, the player with the pebble runs to the base and tries to touch it. If the player makes it to base, the player then becomes IT and the game starts all over again.
